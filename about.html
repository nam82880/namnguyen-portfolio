About

Nam Nguyen is a photojournalist passionate about capturing meaningful local stories with honesty and depth. He’s currently studying photojournalism at the University of Missouri, where he's honing both his technical skills and his thoughtful approach to visual storytelling.

Nam is currently a photo editor with the Columbia Missourian and is building a diverse portfolio of photo essays and news coverage. He’s known for connecting with his subjects and earning their trust and always approaching assignments with empathy and respect.
